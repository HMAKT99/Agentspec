# Code of Conduct

mdpact adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and focus on the work. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

To report a concern, email the maintainers privately (see `SECURITY.md` for the disclosure address; it doubles as the conduct contact until a dedicated address is published).
